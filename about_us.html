About Us

Hi! I'm so glad you're here. My name is Nuha Uddin and I created DESI EATS as a student studying at the New Jersey Institute of Technology
Welcome to my corner of South Asian flavors! I'm passionate about sharing the rich culinary heritage of Bangladesh, Pakistan, and India. Through this website, I've created a platform for food enthusiasts to explore our diverse cuisines and recipes. It brings me joy to see users diving into the site, trying out recipes, and experiencing the vibrant tastes of my culture. Join me on this delicious journey!
